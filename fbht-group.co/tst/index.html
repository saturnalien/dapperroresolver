    About Crest Development Union

    Crest Development Union solves some of the province's biggest challenges by seeing beyond the possible. We support research and the growth of business. We help the startup community build new technology and drive new ideas.

    Our broad scope gives us the perspective to solve some big challenges. We span the full spectrum of sectors and industries – from health care innovations to smart agriculture and food production. From academic research to tech startups and artificial intelligence adoption, from cleantech to renewable energy.

    Crest Development Union fund projects that support the growth and diversity of America’s economy. We provide a range of funding programs targeted to different sectors and development stages. CDU connects local groups across America with the capital and technical know-how to help build inclusive, resilient communities of opportunity.

    CDU provides the emergency assistance that Business desperately need to stay afloat. We will deploy funds to help them bridge the financial gap, and deliver technical assistance to help them navigate the intricate web of public and private resources now available.

    CDU will focus the country, especially those in underserved communities, including entrepreneurs of color, men, women- and veteran-owned businesses that often lack access to flexible, affordable capital.

    How to determine your eligibility

    Each program has its own eligibility criteria based on factors such as the type of program you intent on applying for. Eligibility requirements are clearly indicated on the program details and within the associated program guide

    How to Apply

    Application processes are a little different depending on which program you’re applying for. Follow the application steps outlined on the program page carefully.

    • Funding Program Eligibility Selection

      Select the preferred funding program for your application form.

    • Submit application for Review

      Fill the application carefully and submit for review. CDU uses highly secure systems in order to protect Applicants' information.

    • Review

      The review process is competitive and only the highest quality projects will be considered for funding. If the review is positive, a funding recommendation will be made. Review takes 2 business working days.

    • Approval

      You’ll receive a confirmation mail once your application is approved.

    • Funding

      You will receive instructions on how to receive your support deposit after approval. Successful applicants will execute an agreement with CDU to proceed with the funding.
